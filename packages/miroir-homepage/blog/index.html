Why Miroir? The Philosophy and Rationale

Today's IDEs are paradoxically not that "integrated." They directly address writing and browsing code, but leave out software design, database modeling, CI/CD pipelines, and runtime monitoring. Miroir aims to recapture Smalltalk's interactive development experience while addressing its limitations — bringing development‑time and runtime together in a single, unified environment.
